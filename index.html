Samples of my work

Interactive Map

The purpose of this projet was to install kiosks in a Swiss hospital with touch screens. The web application is an interactive map which helps users to find their directions to the information desk related to the service they are looking for.

We have used Angular 2 to develop the app, Pixi (2D WebGL renderer) for the map and PouchDB for the database.

I have implemented a lot of event trackers via Google Analytics in order to provide reports about users searches or most common actions.

Sport-Ups

Sport-Ups is an outdoor activities booking platform for all sports and nature enthusiasts to share their passions with the world.

The website has been developed with Symfony 2. I have handled most of the frontend development (Twig and Foundation for Sites 6 with SASS and BEM). It was also a first experience for me with Angular 2 and TypeScript.

I have tried to optimize the pages speed and perfomance as much as possible. I also have developed responsive email templates via Foundation for Emails 2.

KLM 30th Anniversary

I've created a Facebook game to celebrate their anniversary in South Korea. The purpose was to invite a friend to book a seat in your virtual plane to try to win a ticket to Amsterdam.

Since it was a Facebook app, I've used a lot of Facebook API functions (login, share, friend list etc..). I've chosen the framework AngularJS to handle the routing, the cookies and many others details like DOM manipulation.

Roughly 13,000 people have participated in this event.
